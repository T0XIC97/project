What’s ITI Courses?

ITI courses stands for Industrial Training Institutes where these are governed by the DGET also known by as Directorate General of Employment and Training. They focus on providing a vocational training to student so that they can get into job opportunities and other small scale level business straightaway and make a good career in it.


These courses are majorly focused to provide employment based skill training and more to the students so that they are industry ready and can go on to become a good industry job oriented person. This also allows them to become ready work forces who can make a good living. Students can opt for ITI courses right after class 10 or even after class 12. There are multiple technical courses offered under this regime just like in the polytechnic fields. These courses are taught by many private and government based centers who specialize in teaching ITI based courses to students.

What are the advantages of going for ITI courses and future scope ?

Industrial Training Institute courses are quite common among a lot of fraternity. These are generally preferred by students in the rural areas as these courses allow them to go for the job-ready position and also gives them something to build their career into. It also makes them ready to earn a living and support their family and more.


Many people from rural areas don’t have that much financial freedom to go for an expensive education. What they want is to have some technical skillset taught that will give them job opportunity and something to make their career good. For them ITI courses are the best way forward. The people with these skillset are often technical field students and have good bit of skillset.


Previously ITI courses were very popular but in the last few years, the popularity has decreased very much, but that is not to say that the course is at all time low. The scope remains far greater for these courses and students can have ready made career with themselves via these courses. They can also go for a readymade industry jobs as these courses gives them wings and multiple knowledge about the field.
